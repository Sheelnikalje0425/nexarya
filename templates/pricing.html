How do payments work?

What if I need changes after launch?

All plans include a 30–60 day support period for bug fixes. Feature additions are quoted separately and are always optional.

Who hosts the project?

We deploy on your own AWS account so you fully own everything. We can also manage it under our account if you prefer.

Can you work with an existing codebase?

Yes. We regularly take over and extend existing projects. Just share what you have and we'll review it before quoting.
