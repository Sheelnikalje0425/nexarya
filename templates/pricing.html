Frequently Asked Questions

Are the prices fixed or negotiable?+
The prices shown are starting points. Your final quote depends on your exact requirements — number of pages, features, complexity, and timeline. We always share a detailed breakdown before any payment.
How long does a project take?+
Simple websites: 1–2 weeks. Web apps with deployment: 3–6 weeks. Full maintenance packages or complex systems: 6–12 weeks. We provide a clear timeline after your first consultation — for free.
Do you require full payment upfront?+
No. We work on a 50% advance, 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, payments are split into milestones so you only pay as work is completed.
Can I add AI chatbot to any plan?+
Yes! AI chatbot integration can be added to any plan as an add-on. The additional cost depends on the complexity and the AI model used. Contact us for a custom quote.
Can I upgrade my plan later?+
Absolutely. All our systems are built with scalability in mind. You can start with the Web Only plan and scale up to deployment or maintenance as your business grows.
What if I need something not listed here?+
Just reach out! We handle web testing, e-commerce platforms, multi-tenant SaaS apps, data dashboards, and more. If you can describe it, we can build it — and we'll quote it fairly.
